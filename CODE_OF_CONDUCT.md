# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We are committed to a welcoming, harassment-free environment for everyone.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and constructive
- Assume good intent
- Focus on technical issues, not personal attacks

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, hateful language
- Doxxing, threats, or sustained disruption

## Enforcement
Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions/comments that violate this code.

## Reporting
Open a private security/disclosure channel via `SECURITY.md` for sensitive concerns.
